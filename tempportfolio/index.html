echoes - Jelly Beach

echoes is a student-run and faculty mentored vertically integrated project at Rochester Institute of Technology. Each year, we examine a theme, and pick apart works from our community to explore how games can foster connections. Our most recent game is Jelly Beach, a child on a boardwalk in the 1960s wants nothing more than a jelly donut!
My role on our Jelly Beach team is the 2D artist/animator as well as a game developer.
